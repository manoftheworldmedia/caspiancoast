Our Story

A bridge
between worlds

Persian roots, California light, Venice soul — and a mural on our wall that says it all.

Caspian Coast Coffee was born from a desire to bring people together through the warmth of coffee and the richness of story.

For me, this isn't just a business — it's a continuation of a journey that began long before I was born.

My family's story is one of courage, resilience, and hope. They crossed oceans and borders to build a new life in California, carrying with them the flavors, traditions, and values of their homeland.

In our kitchen, the aroma of freshly brewed tea and the sound of laughter were constants — reminders that no matter how far we'd traveled, our heritage lived in the everyday moments we shared.

This shop is a celebration of that heritage. It's a place where cultures meet, where recipes and ideas travel as freely as people do, and where every cup tells a story — mine, my family's, and perhaps yours too.

At Caspian Coast Coffee, we aim to create a space that feels both worldly and deeply local: rooted in the vibrant energy of Venice Beach, yet infused with the warmth and hospitality of the Caspian shores. Every detail, from the beans we select to the music we play, is chosen with intention — to honor the past, celebrate the present, and inspire connection.
